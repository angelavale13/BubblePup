About Us

Bubble Pup was founded in January 2020 by four ASU students who love the idea of bringing puppies, dogs, doggos, and furry pals to a cute boba cafe where both owners and pets can enjoy a nice treat. They were inspired by the want to take their own furry friends to a place such as ours, but one did not exist at the time.
